About me

I'm a maths enthusiast with a flair for problem-solving and a passion for bringing mathematics to a wider audience. Beyond numbers, I write code, create music and play sports.

Mathematics

Mathematical Olympiad

I am the Academic Director of the annual Swiss Mathematical Olympiad, a mathematical contest for high schoolers from all around Switzerland. Together with other volunteers, I create exam questions, write teaching material, organise training camps and acompany the best students every year to international competitions.

Research Interest

My main reaseacrh interests are in mathematical logic and computation theory. Topics that fascinate me incldue the interplay of determinism and nondeterminism in Complexity Thoery, as well as the relationship of different models of computation such as Automata, Formal Grammars and the Lambda Calculus.

Mathematical Outreach

I am on a mission to bring the joy of mathematics to a wider audience. With growing complexity of technology and social structures in our society, abstract reasoning and problem solving skills are more important than ever before. My goal is to improve mathematical education in order to reduce the widespread dislike of the subject.
