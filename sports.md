### Ultimate
At the beginning of my undergrad I discovered the sport of Ultimate, a dynamic team sport played with a flying disc
(more commonly known as a Frisbee). Being a non-contact sport, it maximising the technical and tactical aspects
and allows for play with mixed-gendered teams.

### Other Sports

I was captain of the *Trinity College Climbing Club* and member of the *Cambridge University Mountaineering Society*.
I mostly boulder indoors, around the **6B-C** level.

I briefly rowed for the Trinity College Rowing Club *First and Thirds*,
with my main achievement being a first place at the **Talbott Cup 2020**.

I enjoy both long and short distance running (Basler Stadtlauf 2024, 5500m, 25:17).

I play casual chess online, mostly with short time controls, around the **1900 ELO** level.

In high school I dabbled in speed cubing, with my [official](https://www.worldcubeassociation.org/persons/2015IMBA01)
personal best at **29.22 Seconds** for the standard 3x3 cube.
